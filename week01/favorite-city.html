My favourite city is Benin city

Benin City is my favorite city because it is a place rich in history, culture, and tradition. Known as the heart of the ancient Benin Kingdom, the city is home to remarkable art, bronze works, and deep-rooted customs that reflect the pride of its people. Walking through Benin City feels like stepping into a story, from the magnificent Oba's Palace to the lively markets filled with colorful fabrics, local delicacies, and friendly faces. The city beautifully blends the past and present, with modern developments standing alongside historical landmarks. What I love most about Benin City is its warm, welcoming atmosphere and the sense of identity and heritage that its people carry with pride.
